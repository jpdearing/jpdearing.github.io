About Me


I was born and raised in a small town in Texas. I have an absolute passion for cooking, gaming, learning, and now coding. My brother and I discovered our inner nerd while disassembling and reassembling old Tandy computers. Our interest in computers quickly turned into passion, which led us to where we are now.

In 2011 I enrolled in Texas A&M - Corpus Christi's Mechanical Engineering program. This is where my wife and I first met. Of course she was not interested at first, but I can be quite persistent. I finally wore her down enough to marry me in 2017 (she always wanted me, but she'll never admit it!).

As of the creation of this page, my wife is 19 weeks pregnant with our first child, Connor. I am truly excited for this new chapter in our lives, and I hope to one day pass my newfound coding knowledge to my children.
